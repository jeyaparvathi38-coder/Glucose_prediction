    Project Overview
    About GlucoPredict
    A clinical-grade machine learning system for glucose risk stratification, built on the Framingham Heart Study dataset.
    🎯 Problem Statement

    Abnormal blood glucose is a silent epidemic — affecting millions before clinical symptoms appear. Traditional screening is reactive, costly, and inaccessible. GlucoPredict uses longitudinal cardiovascular data to enable proactive, data-driven risk stratification at scale.

    🗂️ Dataset: Framingham Heart Study
    Total Records4,240 patients
    Features15 health biomarkers
    Target VariableBlood glucose level
    Study LocationFramingham, MA, USA
    Task TypeMulti-class Classification
    ML WORKFLOW
    1
    Step 01
    Data Ingestion & EDA
    Loaded framingham.csv, explored distributions of glucose, BMI, age, cholesterol. Identified skewness, outliers, and class imbalance using pandas and matplotlib.
    2
    Step 02
    Data Cleaning & Engineering
    Handled missing values with median imputation. Encoded categoricals. Applied StandardScaler. Created risk tier labels from glucose quantiles.
    3
    Step 03
    Model Training & Tuning
    Trained Random Forest, Logistic Regression, and Decision Tree. Used GridSearchCV with 5-fold cross-validation for hyperparameter optimization.
    4
    Step 04
    Evaluation & Explainability
    Evaluated with ROC-AUC, precision, recall, F1. Applied SHAP TreeExplainer for feature attribution. Random Forest achieved best performance at 87.3%.
